Bags for Better

Every day, we work on our commitment to becoming a more eco‑conscious brand. We are constantly developing new ways to minimize the impact we are having on Mama Earth.

LEARN MORE
+

Recycled Materials

We've developed fabrics and materials that remove plastic bottles from landfills. That's right, our Neoprene Collection linings, our dust bags, and our Nova and Mila styles are made with recycled plastic bottles. 

+

Ethical Manufacturing

Our factory partners abide by strict compliance regulations that outline impeccable standards concerning health, environment, child labor, compensation, and discrimination. We work closely with our partners on a daily basis, with whom we hold long-standing friendships.  

+

Sustainable approach

Moving forward, we want to create for the better. This means eco-friendlier materials, less packaging, and finding second lives for our bags through various initiatives, including Almost Vintage and donating to humans in need.
